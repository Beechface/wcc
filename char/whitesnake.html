Personality

Whitesnake is jovial and upbeat. Despite his eternally-dripping pelt, he's a rather outgoing and friendly tom. He's playful... Especially with what he considers prey. He genuinely believes there is nothing wrong with bloodshed, and is prone to taking things much too far when sparring or training.
