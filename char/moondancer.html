Appearance

Pelt: Medium-furred black-silver tabby with lighter underside
Eyes: Green
Nose & Pawpads: Dark gray

Breed Inspiration: Turkish Angora

Moondancer is of average height and weight, with slightly larger-than-average ears. Her fur is a dark grey base, with tabby stripes. Paler silvery fur frames a delicate face with round green eyes.
