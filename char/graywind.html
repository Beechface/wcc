Appearance

Pelt: Shaded silver tabby with very faint stripes
Eyes: Golden
Nose & Pawpads: Dark gray nose and pawpads
Height: 13" at shoulder
Weight: 14lbs
Body Type: Willowy
Breed Inspiration: Maine Coon

Graywind is a tall yet slender she-cat. Her long, almost wispy fur is white at the base, with a dusting of silvery-gray across her back and shoulders and the outer parts of her legs. Her nose and pawpads are dark. Bright golden eyes stand out in a pretty face, and her ears are tufted.
