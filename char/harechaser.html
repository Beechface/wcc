Appearance

Pelt: Long-furred black and ginger tortoiseshell with a splash of white across her throat
Eyes: Mismatched green and blue
Nose & Pawpads: Split black and pink
Height: 13" at shoulder
Weight: 12lbs
Body Type: Thin side of average
Breed Inspiration: -

Harechaser is of rather average height and build, if a little on the thin side. Her pelt is a striking mix of deep black and vibrant orange, with a small patch of white fur at her throat. Her eyes are mismatched blue and green.

Images

Personality

Shy and more reserved, perhaps overly cautious and tends to overthink. Very uncertain of her place in the world, despite this is also eager to prove that she's as capable of a warrior as her parents and siblings are, knowing that she has large pawsteps to fill with how respected her family is.
