Personality

Dutiful, absolutely devoted to Riverclan's well-being. Has trouble sitting still and is the type to always need to be doing *something* even when told to relax. Generally friendly and affectionate with her clanmates and always willing to lend a helping paw, the recent devastation of Riverclan's warriors has her more determined than ever to pitch in and be useful. More of a hunter than fighter, but won't hesitate to defend anyone she thinks needs defending.
