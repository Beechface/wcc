Personality

Reserved, but not unkind to her clanmates unless she feels that they have wronged her or her family in some way. Her respect and trust are earned and not freely given. Very loyal and driven to prove herself a worthy warrior. Will not shy away from a fight, but she isn't mindless enough to think that fighting is the only thing that matters and is very willing to talk to others and learn from them, too. Even those from the other clans... Though she absolutely will believe that Thunderclan is superior, and that her feral blood sets her apart from even the rest of the clan.
